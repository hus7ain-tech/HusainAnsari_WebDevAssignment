Frequently Asked Questions

Is this course right for a very shy child?

Yes. Our program is specifically designed to be a safe, gentle space. We encourage participation but never force it, allowing children to join in at their own comfort level.

What are the class sizes?

We keep classes small—typically 8-10 students—to ensure every child receives personalized attention and has the opportunity to speak and be heard.

What technology is required?

All you need is a computer or tablet with a camera, a microphone, and a stable internet connection for our live, interactive sessions on Zoom.
